# Privacy Policy - MudraFinance

**Effective Date:** 5th Feb 2026 

**Last Updated:** 5th feb 2026

## Overview
MudraFinance is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension helps you manage personal finances through budget tracking and AI-powered advice.

## Data We Collect

### Financial Information (Stored Locally Only)
- Monthly income amounts
- Expense amounts (rent, utilities, discretionary spending)
- Budget goals and savings targets
- User-defined expense categories and amounts

**Important:** We do NOT collect:
- Credit card numbers
- Bank account information
- Social security numbers
- Transaction histories from financial institutions
- Any personally identifiable information (name, email, address, etc.)

## How We Use Your Data

### Local Storage
- All financial data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API
- Data never leaves your device except when you use the AI Assistant feature
- You maintain complete control and can delete all data anytime

### AI Assistant Feature (Optional)
When you use the AI Assistant:
- Only anonymized budget summaries are sent to Groq AI API (e.g., "income: $5000, total expenses: $3000")
- No detailed transaction data, personal information, or identifiable data is transmitted
- Communication uses encrypted HTTPS
- Groq AI processes requests to provide financial advice and does not store your data

## Data Sharing

**We do NOT:**
- Sell your data to anyone
- Share data with advertisers
- Use data for marketing purposes
- Transfer data to third parties (except Groq AI for AI features as described above)
- Track your browsing history or activity outside the extension

**Third-Party Service:**
- **Groq AI API:** Used only for AI financial advice when you explicitly request it
- Only receives anonymized financial summaries
- Privacy Policy: https://groq.com/privacy-policy/

## Your Rights

- **Access:** All your data is visible within the extension
- **Deletion:** Use "Reset All Data" in Settings to delete everything
- **Control:** Choose whether to use AI features or not
- **Portability:** Your data is stored in standard formats

## Data Security

- Chrome automatically encrypts data stored by the extension
- HTTPS encryption for all API communications
- No server-side storage of your financial information
- No user accounts or authentication required

## Permissions Explanation

- **Storage:** Saves your budget data locally on your device
- **Alarms:** Sends monthly reminders to review your budget


## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted with a new "Last Updated" date.


## Compliance

This extension complies with:
- Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)


---

**Your Privacy Matters:** This extension was designed with privacy as a priority. Your financial data stays on your device, under your control.
